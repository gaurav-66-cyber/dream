About Us

This digital museum was created by me, Gaurav Kunwar — a young student from Butwal, Nepal learning about cyber security and deeply interested in farming.

I wanted to honor our great heroes and martyrs who sacrificed everything for freedom, unity, and justice. Their stories deserve to be remembered forever.

Our Vision

We remember every hero who sacrificed their life, freedom, and comfort so that we could stand here today in peace and dignity.

Their courage, reforms, and ultimate sacrifices built the world we live in. This digital museum keeps their stories alive for future generations.

May their legacy inspire us to be brave, just, and united.
