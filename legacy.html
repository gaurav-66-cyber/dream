About Us

We are young students and developers from Kathmandu who built this digital museum to honor the world's greatest heroes and martyrs. Their sacrifices lit the path of freedom, unity, and justice that we walk today.

This entire website is contained in a single HTML file — pure HTML, CSS & JavaScript. No server needed. You can save it and open it anytime, even without internet.

Our Vision

We remember every hero who sacrificed their life, freedom, and comfort so that we could stand here today in peace and dignity.

Their courage, reforms, and ultimate sacrifices built the world we live in. This digital museum keeps their stories alive for future generations.

May their legacy inspire us to be brave, just, and united.
